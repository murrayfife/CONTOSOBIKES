## Purpose
Used for critical supply components with long lead times or limited suppliers (e-bike batteries, motors, controllers, high-end drivetrains, carbon frames, long-travel forks). Daily period ensures planned orders are generated per day for tight visibility. Aggressive safety margins and longer planning fence ensure supply continuity.
